About

I am a second year PhD student at Inria and affliated to DI ENS in Paris where I am advised by Prof. Laurent Massoulié. My research interest is in online algorithms, inference, and decision-making. Prior to my PhD, I received a M.S. degree in computer science from MIT where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Devavrat Shah. My undergraduate was in economics and computer science as an "élève ingénieur" at Ecole polytechnique. I also had the chance to work with many other inspiring collaborators, among whom are Moïse Blanchard, Prof. Steve Hanneke, Prof. Ali Jadbabie, Prof. Anuran Makur, Amirhossein Reisizadeh, and Prof. Laurent Viennot.
