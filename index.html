About

I am a data scientist at University of California Berkeley. I am working with researchers in Innovation and Business Analystics on machine learning projects applied to patents and scientific publications. I am also supervising Engineering Master students on Machine Learning projects (such as NLP or time series predictions projects) related to the field of metaresearch. I graduated in 2021 with a Master degree in Optimization from UC Berkeley. as well as a Master degree in Artificial Intelligence from CentraleSupelec. Before that, I also completed a bachelor of Engineering at CentraleSupelec.
